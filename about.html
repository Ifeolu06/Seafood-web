Order & Delivery

Shopping with Mod is easy! You can get your fresh fish and seafood delivered to any location within Lagos without any hassle. Fresh fish and seafood ordered by 12 noon can be delivered to your door on the same day or within 24 hours (for special orders that need to be sourced separately).
We offer FREE delivery service from Monday to Saturday for all orders over N20,000 within a 5 km radius of Q-brat Zoo, so why not ask friends and family to share a seafood order with you?
